# Procurement Module Map (Zenvix Procurement)

## Purpose
A source-to-pay operational layer managing supplier relationships, contract compliance, and rigorous purchase governance to ensure efficient business spending.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Operations
- **Requisition Desk** (/core/procurement/prs)
  - **Button**: "Create Purchase Request"
  - **Purpose**: Intake for departmental goods/services requests with budget validation.
- **PO Release Desk** (/core/procurement/po-release)
  - **Purpose**: Formal governance layer for releasing Purchase Orders to vendors after approvals.

### 2. Supplier Network
- **Supplier Desk** (/core/procurement/suppliers)
  - **Button**: "Supplier Onboarding"
  - **Purpose**: Master directory for managing vendor relationships and performance data.
- **Supplier Portal** (/core/procurement/portal)
  - **Purpose**: External-facing desk for supplier interaction and document submission.

### 3. Governance
- **Contract Desk** (/core/procurement/contracts)
  - **Button**: "New Service Contract"
  - **Purpose**: Legal agreement repository with lifecycle alerts and SLA tracking.
- **Risk and Compliance** (/core/procurement/risk)
  - **Purpose**: Monitoring vendor risk factors, compliance certifications, and security vetting.

### 4. Intelligence
- **Spend Intelligence** (/core/procurement/insights)
  - **Purpose**: ROI analysis and spend categorization dashboards to optimize procurement strategy.

## Core Integrations
- **Finance**: Direct linkage for accounts payable generation upon PO fulfillment.
- **Legal**: Contract vetting and regulatory compliance audits.
- **Audit**: Immutable history of purchase approvals and supplier vetting steps.
