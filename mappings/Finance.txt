# Zenvix Finance & Core Services Map

## Purpose
A strategic and operational control center for the enterprise, providing financial governance, secure communications, and real-time risk mitigation.

## 1. Finance Module (/core/finance)
Core ledger and operational treasury engine.
- **Money Desk** (/moneydesk): Centralized work queue for approvals and financial alerts.
- **Treasury Map** (/treasury): Global visualization of liquidity and asset distribution.
- **Ledger Core** (/ledger): Double-entry accounting master record with "New Journal Entry" flow.
- **Invoice Capture** (/invoices): OCR and manual intake for document processing.
- **Assets & Capex** (/assets): Fixed asset tracking, depreciation runs, and Capex budget approvals.
- **Receivables & Payables** (/receivables | /payables): AR/AP lifecycle automation from intake to settlement.
- **Finance Insights** (/insights): KPI dashboards for revenue, runway, and expense analysis.
- **Payroll Execution**: Localized payroll calculation and disbursement (ID, SG, AE).

## 2. Payment Module (/core/payment)
High-volume transaction execution and routing engine.
- **Transaction Flow**: Core execution, approval, rejection, and settlement.
- **Routing & Policies**: Dynamic routing of transactions to multiple providers (Provider Health Sweeps).
- **Refunds & Disputes**: Dispute management with evidence packing and automated resolution paths.
- **Settlement Engine**: Daily batch reconciliation and movement to money sources.
- **Device Management**: Tracking and status updates for payment hardware devices.

## 3. Chat Module (/core/chat)
Secure enterprise communication system.
- **Multi-Type Rooms**: Direct, Group, Department, Role, and Company-wide channels.
- **Message Integrity**: Sent, Delivered, and Read status tracking per message.
- **Scalable Unread Sync**: Real-time unread counts with cursor-based historical pagination.
- **Global Search**: Searchable thread history across all joined rooms.

## 4. Mail Module (/core/mail)
Internal secure transmission system.
- **Intel Exchange**: Secure internal mail accounts (`@zenvix.internal`).
- **Threading Engine**: Thread-based message grouping for unified intel streams.
- **Folders**: Inbox, Sent, Drafts, Starred, and Trash management.
- **Real-time Alerting**: Integration with System Notifications for new transmissions.

## 5. Notification Module (Shared Interface)
Real-time organizational alert fabric.
- **Global Broadcast**: Real-time WebSocket delivery of system alerts (Finance, HR, Security).
- **Unread Strategy**: Centralized unread counts across Mail, Chat, and System notifications.
- **Actionable Links**: Every notification is linked to its source entity or workflow path.

## 6. Antifraud Engine (Automation Layer)
Real-time anomaly detection and risk mitigation.
- **Event Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring of the Zenvix Global Event Fabric.
- **Anomaly Detection**: AI-triggered analysis (`AI_ANALYZE`) for high-value mutations (e.g., >100M IDR payroll).
- **Risk Signaling**: Automated generation of Risk Signals in Procurement and Finance alerts.
- **Compliance Audit**: Every detection is logged to the Immutable Audit Vault for governance.

## Core Integrations
- **HR**: Payroll disbursements and employee cost center syncing.
- **Procurement**: Automatic payable generation and risk assessment.
- **Inventory/Retail**: COGS management and sales revenue settlement.
- **Security**: Immutable audit logging and context-aware permissions.
