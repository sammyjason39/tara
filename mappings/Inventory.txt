# Inventory Module Map

## Purpose
The Inventory module provides real-time visibility and governance over physical and digital stock across all warehouse and retail locations. It ensures stock accuracy, manages replenishments, and tracks audits.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Stock Monitoring (Dashboard)
- **Path**: /core/inventory/dashboard
- **Button**: "Run Low Stock Scan" / "Run Expiry Scan"
- **Purpose**: To proactively identify stock-outs or expiring goods.
- **Actions**:
  - Metrics: Total Items, Stock Valuation, On-hand Qty.
  - Alerts: High-severity low stock and expiry warnings.
  - Trigger: System scan buttons.
  - Result: Updated alert queue for operational intervention.

### 2. Physical Receiving
- **Path**: /core/inventory/receiving
- **Button**: "New Receipt" / "Sync Receipt"
- **Purpose**: To register incoming goods from suppliers or internal transfers.
- **Actions**:
  - Input: PO reference, actual vs. expected quantity, condition.
  - Trigger: "Confirm Receipt"
  - Approval: Warehouse Manager sign-off.
  - Result: Immediate increase in on-hand quantity; Finance reconciliation triggered.

### 3. Adjustments & Audits
- **Path**: /core/inventory/adjustments
- **Button**: "Create Adjustment"
- **Purpose**: To correct stock discrepancies found during cycle counts or damaged goods.
- **Actions**:
  - Input: Reason (Damaged, Lost, Found), quantity change.
  - Validation: Mandatory reason code.
  - Trigger: "Submit for Approval"
  - Approval: Finance verification if valuation change exceeds threshold.
  - Result: Adjusted stock balance + immutable audit log entry.

### 4. Visibility (Stock Hub)
- **Path**: /core/inventory/stock
- **Button**: "View Item Details"
- **Purpose**: A centralized searchable database of all items in the inventory.
- **Features**: Filter by location, department, or module contribution (e.g., Retail).

## Core Integrations
- **Sales/Retail**: Real-time stock reservation and deduction upon sale.
- **Finance**: COGS tracking and stock valuation reporting.
- **Procurement**: Inbound shipment tracking from Purchase Orders.
- **Audit**: Every stock movement is logged with user and location context.
