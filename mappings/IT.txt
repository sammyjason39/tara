# IT Module Map (Zenvix IT Workspace)

## Purpose
Governance of digital identities, hardware asset lifecycle tracking, and network health monitoring to ensure high availability and security across all tenant locations.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Accounts
- **Account Desk** (/core/it/accounts)
  - **Button**: "Manage Access"
  - **Purpose**: Centralized console for provisioning and de-provisioning user accounts and platform permissions.
  - **Workflow**: Often triggered by HR hiring/termination events.

### 2. Assets
- **Device Desk** (/core/it/devices)
  - **Button**: "Assign Device"
  - **Purpose**: Mapping physical hardware (Laptops, Mobile POS, LAN Nodes) to users and locations.
  - **Actions**: Scan serial numbers, update device status, and track physical location history.

### 3. Health
- **System Health** (/core/it/health)
  - **Button**: "Monitor Status"
  - **Purpose**: Real-time observability platform for monitoring LAN edge nodes, network latency, and service availability.
  - **Workflow**: Automated alerts for connectivity drops or hardware failures.

## Core Integrations
- **HR**: Employee lifecycle events trigger automatic identity management.
- **Payment**: Device mapping ensures encrypted and secure routing for physical payment hardware.
- **Inventory**: Tracking physical stock levels of hardware assets.
- **Audit**: All access mutations and device movements are logged for security compliance.
