# Marketing Module Map

## Purpose
The Marketing module manages multi-channel campaigns, target audience segmentation, and ROI analysis to drive brand awareness and lead generation.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Campaign Planning
- **Path**: Dashboard → Campaigns → Create
- **Button**: "New Campaign"
- **Purpose**: To design a marketing initiative with budget and objectives.
- **Actions**:
  - Input: Campaign name, channel (Email, Social, SMS), budget, start/end dates.
  - Trigger: "Submit"
  - Approval: HOD (Head of Marketing).
  - Result: Active campaign project in the database.

### 2. Campaign Execution
- **Path**: Dashboard → Campaign Execution
- **Button**: "Launch / Execute"
- **Purpose**: Operationalizing the content and reaching the target audience.
- **Actions**:
  - Input: Audience segmentation, creative assets, scheduling.
  - Validation: Target list completeness.
  - Trigger: "Execute"
  - Automation: Automatic lead hand-off to the Sales module.
  - Result: Outbound communication triggered + automated logs.

### 3. ROI Analytics
- **Path**: Dashboard → Analytics
- **Button**: "Generate Report"
- **Purpose**: Measuring the success of marketing spend against lead conversion ratios.
- **Actions**:
  - Inputs: Filter by campaign, date, or channel.
  - Output: Conversion rates, cost-per-lead, engagement metrics.
  - Accuracy: Data synced in real-time from the Sales funnel.
  - Result: Actionable insights for future budget requests.

## Core Integrations
- **Sales**: Direct hand-off of qualified leads.
- **Finance**: Budget utilization and marketing spend tracking.
- **IT**: Management of marketing automation tools and API keys.
