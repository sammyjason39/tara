# Payment Module Map (Zenvix Payment Workspace)

## Purpose
The enterprise execution layer for financial transfers, multi-bank settlement, refunds, and dispute management. It provides a highly secure and auditable environment for all transactional data.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Execution
- **Payment Command Center** (/core/payment/dashboard)
  - **Purpose**: Real-time cockpit for monitoring success rates, transfer volumes, and operational alerts.
- **Execution Hub** (/core/payment/execution)
  - **Button**: "New Batch Execution"
  - **Purpose**: Manual and automated processing of high-volume financial transfers and bank instructions.
- **Refund Desk** (/core/payment/refunds)
  - **Button**: "Process Request"
  - **Purpose**: Dedicated lifecycle management for credit/debit card refunds and wire reversals.

### 2. Routing
- **Provider Routing** (/core/payment/providers)
  - **Button**: "Configure Gateway"
  - **Purpose**: Logic-based routing across multiple payment gateways (Stripe, Adyen, Bank Direct) to optimize fees and reliability.
- **Device Routing** (/core/payment/devices)
  - **Purpose**: Mapping physical card readers and POS hardware to specific logical payment lanes and locations.

### 3. Governance
- **Disputes & Chargebacks** (/core/payment/disputes)
  - **Button**: "Submit Evidence"
  - **Purpose**: Centralized console for responding to credit card disputes with automated evidence gathering.
- **Audit & Evidence** (/core/payment/audit)
  - **Purpose**: Immutable vault for transaction logs, cryptographic proofs, and settlement records for forensic audit.

## Core Integrations
- **Finance**: Direct ledger linkage for automated reconciliation of settled funds.
- **IT**: Device inventory management for physical payment hardware.
- **Security**: Contextual encryption and hardware-level security module (HSM) logic.
- **Retail**: Real-time authorization for physical store transactions.
