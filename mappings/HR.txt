# HR Module Map (Zenvix HR OS)

## Purpose
An enterprise-grade Human Resources Operating System managing the complete employee lifecycle with a focus on human-centric workflows, granular security, and auditability.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Dashboard (PulseDesk)
- **Path**: /core/hr
- **Button**: "PulseDesk"
- **Purpose**: Centralized view of workforce metrics, alerts, and HR KPIs.

### 2. People Management
- **Path**: /core/hr/roster (People Directory)
- **Button**: "People Directory"
- **Purpose**: Master roster of employees with 360-degree profiles.
- **Workflow**: 
  - Path: /core/hr/people/:userId
  - Purpose: Detailed employee profile management and lifecycle history.

### 3. Hiring & Talent Flow (Recruitment OS)
- **Path**: /core/hr/talent (Talent)
- **Button**: "Talent Dashboard"
- **Features**:
  - **AI Job Description Hub**: Automated drafting with tone control and market alignment benchmarking.
  - **Smart Talent Sourcing**: Talent lead ingestion and automated matching against job requirements.
  - **Onboarding Pipeline**: Seamless lead-to-candidate conversion and document collection.

### 4. Organization & Planning
- **Path**: /core/hr/org-map (Org Structure)
- **Button**: "Org Structure"
- **Strategic Planning**:
  - **Headcount Modeler**: Creating fiscal-year scenarios and headcount growth plans.
  - **What-If Analysis**: Comparing budget impact of different hiring scenarios.
  - **Succession Planning**: Modeling successors for critical roles and assessing leadership bench strength.

### 5. Performance & Growth (Talent Development)
- **Path**: /core/hr/growth (Performance)
- **Button**: "Performance Desk"
- **AI Capabilities**:
  - **Career Pathing**: AI-generated role suggestions and readiness index forecasting.
  - **Mentorship Marketplace**: Skill-based mentor matching and developmental pairing.
  - **Learning Personalization**: Automated gap-filler enrollment and training ROI analytics.
  - **Skills Inventory**: Global skill ontology and profile proficiency verification.
  - **Talent Marketplace**: Internal internal search based on skill requirements.

### 6. Payroll & Compensation (Financial HR)
- **Path**: /core/hr/paycycle (Payroll)
- **Button**: "Payroll Desk"
- **Features**:
  - **Multi-Currency Consolidation**: Reporting in local and base currencies (USD/Global).
  - **Exchange Management**: Centralized rate tracking for multi-national payroll.
  - **Labor Cost Projection**: Future staff cost forecasting (existing vs. planned).
  - **Budget Variance**: AI alerts for potential departmental budget overruns.
  - **Total Rewards**: Consolidated statements (Salary + Benefits) for employees.

### 7. Compliance & Governance (Compliance OS)
- **Path**: /core/hr/lexboard (Compliance Center)
- **Button**: "Compliance Center"
- **Sub-sections**:
  - Smart Vault: OCR document management
  - Compliance Engine: Per-country module calculations
    - Indonesia: BPJS Kesehatan, BPJS Ketenagakerjaan, PPh21
    - Singapore: CPF
    - UAE: WPS
  - Export Center: CSV, Excel, XML, PDF downloads
  - Government Portals: DJP Online, BPJS Employer Portal
  - AI Suggestions: Automated compliance module recommendations

### 8. Intelligence
- **Path**: /core/hr/insights (Insights)
- **Button**: "HR Insights"
- **Predictive Engine**: 
  - **Turnover Forecasting**: Churn probability modeling and flight risk detection.
  - **Performance Predictor**: Probability analysis for goal achievement and intervention recommendations.

### 9. Approvals & Portal
- **Path**: /core/hr/flowgate (Approval Center)
- **Button**: "Approval Center"
- **Purpose**: Centralized inbox for all HR lifecycle approval requests.
- **Path**: /core/hr/portal (My Pulse)
- **Button**: "Employee Portal"
- **Purpose**: Employee self-service for payslips, leave requests, and profile updates.

### 10. Time & Attendance
- **Path**: /core/hr/time
- **Button**: "Time & Tracker"
- **Features**:
  - Leave Management
  - Attendance Tracking
  - Holiday Calendar
  - Shift Scheduling
- **Integrations**: Syncs with payroll and employee portal.

## HR Command Model
Defined system commands executed through the CommandBus and handled by domain services:
- **General**: `HireEmployeeCommand`, `PromoteEmployeeCommand`, `TransferEmployeeCommand`, `TerminateEmployeeCommand`, `SuspendEmployeeCommand`
- **Recruitment**: `CreateJobOpeningCommand`, `ConvertLeadToCandidateCommand`, `ScheduleInterviewCommand`
- **Payroll**: `ExecutePayrollCommand`, `AdjustCompensationCommand`, `GeneratePayslipCommand`
- **Compliance**: `GenerateComplianceReportCommand`, `ExportGovernmentReportCommand`, `EnableComplianceModuleCommand`

## HR Event Model
Domain events published to the Global Event Bus:
- **Employee**: `employee.created`, `employee.promoted`, `employee.transferred`, `employee.suspended`, `employee.terminated`
- **Recruitment**: `candidate.converted`, `interview.scheduled`
- **Payroll**: `payroll.executed`, `payslip.generated`
- **Compliance**: `compliance.report.generated`, `compliance.module.enabled`
