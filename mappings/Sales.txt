# Sales Module Map (Zenvix CRM & Sales)

## Purpose
A full-lifecycle sales operating system from lead acquisition and pipeline management to quote approvals, orders, and executive forecasting.

## Navigation & Workflows

### 1. Operations
- **Sales Dashboard** (/core/sales/dashboard)
  - **Purpose**: Real-time KPI cockpit (Leads, Deals, Conversion Rates).
- **Leads** (/core/sales/leads)
  - **Button**: "New Lead"
  - **Purpose**: Capture and score incoming prospects.
- **Pipeline Board** (/core/sales/pipeline)
  - **Purpose**: Visual Kanban board for tracking deal stages.
- **Opportunities** (/core/sales/opps)
  - **Button**: "Convert to Opp"
  - **Purpose**: High-intent deal management with revenue value and closing dates.
- **Quote Desk** (/core/sales/quotes)
  - **Button**: "Generate Professional Quote"
  - **Purpose**: Formalizing business proposals with multi-step approval gates.
- **Timeline** (/core/sales/timeline)
  - **Purpose**: Chronological view of all customer interactions (calls, meetings, emails).
- **Sales Orders** (/core/sales/orders)
  - **Button**: "Formalize Order"
  - **Purpose**: Binding agreement generation and direct integration with Finance/Inventory.

### 2. Leadership
- **Manager View** (/core/sales/manager)
  - **Purpose**: Oversight of team performance, activities, and workload distribution.
- **Executive Forecast** (/core/sales/forecast)
  - **Purpose**: Predictive revenue modeling based on pipeline probability and weighted deal values.
- **Audit Log** (/core/sales/audit)
  - **Purpose**: Immutable history of deal mutations, status changes, and user actions.

## Core Integrations
- **Marketing**: Attribution tracking from campaigns to leads.
- **Finance**: Automated accounts receivable (AR) and invoice generation upon order.
- **Inventory**: Real-time stock reservation for physical goods.
- **Comms**: Integration with ChatHub and internal bulletin systems for deal alerts.
