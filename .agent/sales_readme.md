# Zenvix Core — Sales Workspace (Locked)

## 1. Overview

The **Sales Workspace** is the operational environment where sales teams manage:

- Qualified leads from Marketing
- Opportunities and pipeline execution
- Daily follow-ups and deal progression
- Quotes, approvals, negotiations, closing
- Full CRM communication timeline

It is designed to be:

- Human-centric (daily usable workspace)
- AI-assisted (next-best-action + forecasting)
- Auditable (enterprise-grade logging)
- Secure (minimal external dependencies)
- Integrated deeply with all Zenvix Core modules

Sales is not a standalone CRM addon — it is a **core operational engine** inside Zenvix Business OS.

---

## 2. Core Goals

1. Provide a true daily workspace for sales representatives
2. Eliminate lead loss through SLA enforcement and automation
3. Deliver CRM-level pipeline and contact management natively
4. Integrate directly with Marketing, Finance, Inventory, Payments
5. Reduce dependency on external CRMs (HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce)
6. Provide AI-driven forecasting and risk monitoring

---

## 3. Core Responsibilities

Sales Workspace owns:

- Lead acceptance and response execution
- Opportunity lifecycle management
- Customer communication and activity logging
- Quote generation + approval routing
- Pipeline forecasting and performance analytics

Marketing generates demand.
Sales converts demand into revenue.

---

## 4. Daily Sales Workflow (Operational)

### Step 1 — Lead Reception

- Marketing pushes qualified leads into Sales in real time
- AI assigns priority and recommended owner

### Step 2 — Immediate Action

- Rep receives:
  - Notification
  - SLA timer
  - Suggested first response

### Step 3 — Pipeline Execution

- Lead progresses through stages:
  - New → Contacted → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed

### Step 4 — Quote + Approval

- Quotes generated inside Zenvix
- Routed for approvals (Finance, HOD)

### Step 5 — Closing + Feedback Loop

- Deal closes → Finance + Payment triggered
- Marketing receives attribution feedback
